# Mobile Panchangam Image Share Design Specification (v2 - Multilingual & Complete)

## Overview
This document specifies the updated design for sharing the complete Daily Panchangam details as a single, high-resolution PNG image card styled in a **Traditional Daily Wall Calendar (தினசரி நாள்காட்டி)** theme, fully localized to the user's selected language (Tamil, English, Hindi, Kannada, Telugu, Malayalam).

## User Intent & Requirements
- **Primary Goal**: Single-tap "Share as Image" button on `PanchangamPage`.
- **Theme**: Traditional Wall Calendar aesthetic (deep red/saffron banner `#b71c1c`, parchment background `#fffdf8`, gold borders, crisp structured tables).
- **100% Complete Contents**:
  1. **Header Banner**: Invocation, Date, Tamil/Solar Date, Weekday, Location, Sunrise/Sunset/Moonrise times.
  2. **Core 5 Limbs (பஞ்சாங்கம்)**: Thithi, Nakshatra, Yogam, **Karanam** (with end times), Rashi, Chandrastamam Nakshatras.
  3. **Badges**: Subha Muhurtham Status, Vasthu Day & Puja Neram, Agni Nakshathiram, Netram / Jeevan.
  4. **Auspicious Timings**: Nalla Neram, Gowri Nalla Neram, **Nakshatra Vara Yogams (🌟)**, **Abhijit Muhurtham (☀️)**.
  5. **Inauspicious Timings**: Rahu Kalam, Yamagandam, Gulika Kalam.
  6. **24 Horai Table**: Complete 24 Day & Night Horais grid with localized planet names and color badges.
  7. **Footer Branding**: `Generated by https://tinyurl.com/drik-vedic`.
- **Dynamic Localization**: All labels, headings, element names, and instructions must adapt 100% dynamically based on `settings.language` (`t(key, settings.language)`).

---

## Component Architecture

### `PanchangamShareCard` Component
- Positioned in a hidden off-screen wrapper (`position: absolute; left: -9999px`).
- Captures cleanly with `html2canvas` at 2x resolution scale (`scale: 2`).
- Width: `1080px` fixed layout to ensure perfect rendering across all mobile browsers.
